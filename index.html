Holy Angel University Street Dance Crew (HAUSDC) is a talented group of dancers from the prestigious Holy Angel University. Established to showcase their skills, they have captivated audiences with their vibrant performances and dedication to the art of dance.
HAUSDC proudly took part in the prestigious Dance Supremacy 2023 competition. Their journey through intense rehearsals, creative brainstorming, and exhilarating performances highlighted their dedication to the art of dance. This event served as a platform for them to showcase their skills and connect with fellow dance enthusiasts, representing their school with pride.
